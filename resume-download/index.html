INDIVIDUAL WEBSITE


The storyline for the website follows campus cats as the main characters and spreads awareness of their positive influence on campus and students’ lives. The website highlights the names of the cats and serves as a relaxing and comfort place for all the local cat lovers.


COMICS

“Saeed Was Here” is the story about an Emirati boy ghost named Saeed who lives in the library at NYU Abu Dhabi. Saeed used to be a young boy from the UAE, but now he's a mischievous spirit who likes to scare students away from his favorite bookshelves. “Saeed Was Here” is a cheerful story with the spooky elements portrayed in the artistic and interactive elements of the comics, including sound, selection of the desired ending, and easy navigation when reading the comics. All of these elements create funny and spooky vibe, engaging the reader’s attention starting from the first page.


SOUND PROJECT

The sound project was inspired by the social media trends of ASMR and Mukbangs to depict an engaging online experience akin to a TV game show. Typically ASMR represents trying to come up with different sounds to make the listener feel a ticklish feeling, while Mukbangs are generally eating on camera. The team wanted to combine the audio-entertaining components of both to create the “Late Night Guess the Sound” quiz.



VIDEO PROJECT

Arts Center Heist is a depiction of an interactive experience of a potential heist scenario that happened at an exhibition. This project portrays a sense of adventure, action, and comedy. The project’s idea was inspired by the constant exhibitions that take place on campus Arts Center every day and the Spanish TV series called La Casa de Papel or Money Heist. This video project allows the viewers to imagine themselves as thieves of a powerful artifact - things, that don’t happen often.
